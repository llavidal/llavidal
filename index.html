LLAVIDAL vs. Standard LLVMs: Understanding Complex Human-Object Interactions

Abstract

Large Language Vision Models (LLVMs) effectively process internet videos but often struggle with the nuanced dynamics of Activities of Daily Living (ADL). LLAVIDAL introduces a new framework for creating multiview ADL datasets, incorporating 3D poses, object trajectories, and action-conditioned insights to improve LLVM performance in ADL contexts. Our novel ADL-X dataset and the ADLMCQ benchmark quantify the enhanced ADL comprehension provided by LLAVIDAL, which outperforms existing models in temporal reasoning for complex ADLs.
